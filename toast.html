Aquamichi

During my undergrad junior biomimicry studio course, I imagined how humans could swim in air. This idea manifested itself into a swimming simulator.

Industrial Design is Dead

I gave a presentation to a room of industrial design students that industrial design is dead. The industrial design industry lacks diversity and the education is out-dated.

Echo

I had an awesome hands-on experience designing lighting and furniture pieces from scratch. I was introduced to the fulfilling process of bringing a sketch to life, something I have carried over to my digital product design career!

Qohi

Qohi is a coffee app that provides small local coffee shops with a digital platform that can help their businesses compete against big coffee chains’ digital advantages.

Tokyo 2020

Food is undoubtedly a large part of sports culture. In honor of the Tokyo 2020 games, I designed a food menu and branding inspired by the Olympic rings.

Revulve

A speculative design project that reinvents the space and experience of airplanes while questioning: “How contactless can I go with this?” Sometimes, pushing the limits can lead to absurd ideas, but it’s also an imperative part of the creative problem solving process.

Humana

I developed a series of devices as manifestations of what it means to monitor our health. The goal of Humana is to reimagine and reinvent human life through sight, sound, touch, and function.
